Steampilot

Steampilot is a personal collection of patches and mod tweaks for different
customizations in KSP.

Included in this folder:
- Steampilot_RSEBlueDog.cfg
	Redirects stock engine sounds in Rocket Sound Enhancement (RSE)
	to BlueDog sound clips stored under Steampilot/Sounds/BlueDog.
- Steampilot_BDB_StockFuel.cfg
	Converts BlueDog liquid engines to stock LiquidFuel/Oxidizer.

Requirements:
- RocketSoundEnhancement
- RocketSoundEnhancementDefault
- BlueDog (for license/source context of the copied sound pack)

Install:
- Keep folder as GameData/Steampilot
- No other action needed
